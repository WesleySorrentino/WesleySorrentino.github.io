This is a simple To-Do list Application

To use the application:

  • Simply type into the text box that says "Add New ToDo".
  • To hide the text box, Click on the plus icon.
  • To check off items, simply click on item in the list.
  • To Delete an item, hover to the left of the item and click on the trash icon.
